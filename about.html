Eduardo Gooda is a Brazilian Designer & Art Director based in São Paulo and working remotely worldwide.

His services helped build branding solutions, illustrations systems & visual directions for products, brands and animations.

In his 8+ years of experience, Gooda collaborated with big ad agencies and design studios where he worked alongside clients such as Google, Apple, Airbnb, Facebook, Mastercard among many other.

His most recent experiences includes being an Art Director at Buck and previously at R/GA.
