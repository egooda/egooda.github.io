This is the archive of Eduardo Gooda's work featuring selected projects & visual explorations of his 8+ years experience working on the design and advertising industry.

About

Gooda is a Brazilian Designer & Art Director based in São Paulo and working remotely worldwide.

His services encompass building branding solutions, illustration systems & visual directions for products, brands and animations.

Experience

Working at big advertising agencies and design studios, he collaborated alongside clients such as Google, Apple, Airbnb, Meta, Mastercard among many others.

His most recents work experiences include working as an Art Director at BUCK and previously at R/GA.

Freevee

From the shape to the brand’s personality on and offline.

Sesame Workshop
