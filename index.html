Devanescente

This is my final senior project that consists in a study of self-knowledge printed as a experimental handbook. The book was full filled with random intimate thoughts and personal illustrations.

Postio

This project came out when I tried to send my gf a postcard. I then discovered how unnecessarily complicated it was and thought about building a platform where you could buy, build and send them at the same place.

Magnetis

At my current job, I have the opportunity to do some illustrations projects to work with my UI designs. These projects consists in redesign small sections and create new things to work with the product.

Debut Album

Some very old friends finally started their shelved project of creating a indie band named Koalas. My job was to create their debut album cover representing their songs mood, some chill out experimental jams.

Experiments

This is a collection of illustrations I did as studies and side projects by trying to improve my skills and learn new painting styles. Some of them got sold as a serie of individual prints for small frames.
